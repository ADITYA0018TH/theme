# Design System: The Crimson Vine - Table Ordering
**Project ID:** 7290068149301289988

## 1. Visual Theme & Atmosphere
**Aesthetic Philosophy: "The Digital Concierge"**
The theme is sophisticated, appetizing, and minimalist. It moves away from "utility-first" ordering toward a "luxury-service" feel. High-contrast typography and generous whitespace are used to create a sense of "Quiet Luxury." The mood is warm and inviting, like a candlelit restaurant.

## 2. Color Palette & Roles
* **Deep Onyx (#000000)**: Used for the primary navigation header and high-authority text. Represents stability and premium branding.
* **Crimson Flame (#f96d00)**: The primary action color. Used for buttons, price tags, and high-energy interactive elements. 
* **Bone White (#fafafa)**: The base surface color. Provides a clean, modern canvas that mimics high-end stationery.
* **Muted Clay (#e1e1e1)**: Used for input borders and subtle section dividers.

## 3. Typography Rules
* **Headings**: bold, high-contrast sans-serif (e.g., Poppins or Inter). High tracking on small labels.
* **Body**: Clean, readable sans-serif with generous line-height for easy reading on mobile devices.
* **Letter-spacing**: Tight for large headings (-0.02em) and loose for metadata (+0.05em).

## 4. Component Stylings
* **Buttons**: Pill-shaped or 12px rounded corners. Gradient fill from #f96d00 to #e06200. Soft ambient glow on hover.
* **Cards/Containers**: 16px rounded corners. No hard borders; use subtle background shifts (#f5f5f5) for separation.
* **Inputs/Forms**: Underline-only or 12px rounded wells. 1.5px stroke weight. Focus state adds a 4px soft orange glow.

## 5. Layout Principles
* **Mobile-First**: Single-column vertical stacking is the default. 
* **Whitespace**: Use "Luxury Whitespace" (32px-48px) between major sections.
* **Sticky Elements**: Floating "Order Summary" or "Place Order" button at the bottom of the viewport for constant accessibility.
